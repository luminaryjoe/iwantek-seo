### Q8: How do I clean my call center headset?

**Direct Answer**: Clean your headset weekly using 70% isopropyl alcohol wipes for the headband and microphone, and mild soap solution for ear cushions.

**Cleaning Protocol**:

**Daily (End of Shift)**:
- Wipe microphone boom with alcohol wipe (70% isopropyl)
- Quick wipe of headband padding
- Time required: 30 seconds

**Weekly (Deep Clean)**:
1. **Remove ear cushions** (twist and pull)
2. **Clean cushions**: Warm water + mild soap, air dry 2 hours
3. **Wipe headband**: Alcohol wipe, let dry 1 minute
4. **Clean microphone**: Alcohol wipe on foam windscreen
5. **Sanitize connectors**: Cotton swab with alcohol
6. **Reassemble**: Ensure cushions fully dry before reattaching

**Monthly**:
- Inspect cable for fraying
- Check Quick Disconnect mechanism
- Test all call control buttons

**Important**: Never submerge headset in liquid. Never use bleach or ammonia-based cleaners.

**Health Note**: Regular cleaning reduces bacterial count by 99.7% (CDC guidelines for shared equipment).

**Source**: CDC Guidelines for Cleaning Electronic Devices; Wantek Product Care Manual
