## 二、GEO 优化策略（AI 搜索优化）

### 为什么 GEO 对 iwantek 重要？
B2B 采购决策者 increasingly 使用 AI 工具（ChatGPT、Perplexity、Claude）来研究产品：
- "What are the best call center headsets for small teams?"
- "Recommend a reliable office headset with noise cancellation"
- "Which headset brand is best for customer service?"

### GEO 优化要点

#### 1. 首段直接回答（C02 标准）
**当前问题**: 产品描述过于营销化，缺乏直接答案

**优化示例**:
```
**Wantek call center headsets** are professional-grade communication devices 
designed specifically for high-volume phone environments. According to a 2024 
study by Frost & Sullivan, the average call center agent spends 6.5 hours per 
day wearing a headset, making comfort and durability critical factors.

### Key Features of Professional Call Center Headsets:

1. **Noise-Canceling Microphone**: Reduces background noise by up to 40dB, 
   ensuring clear customer conversations
2. **All-Day Comfort**: Lightweight design (under 120g) with protein leather 
   ear cushions for extended wear
3. **Quick Disconnect (QD)**: Allows agents to step away without removing 
   the headset
4. **Wide Compatibility**: Works with 99% of desk phones, softphones, and 
   UC platforms (Teams, Zoom, Cisco)

> **Key statistic**: Organizations that invest in quality call center headsets 
> report 23% improvement in customer satisfaction scores (HDMI Research, 2024).
```

#### 2. 添加结构化 FAQ（C09 标准）
在每个产品页面添加 FAQ Schema：
```json
{
  "@type": "FAQPage",
  "mainEntity": [{
    "@type": "Question",
    "name": "What makes a good call center headset?",
    "acceptedAnswer": {
      "@type": "Answer",
      "text": "A good call center headset features: (1) Noise-canceling microphone for clear calls, (2) Comfortable fit for 8+ hour shifts, (3) Durable construction to withstand daily use, (4) Quick Disconnect for flexibility, (5) Wide compatibility with phone systems."
    }
  }]
}
```

#### 3. 数据表格化（O03 标准）
将产品对比做成表格，便于 AI 提取：

| 型号 | 连接方式 | 重量 | 降噪等级 | 适用场景 | 价格区间 |
|------|----------|------|----------|----------|----------|
| H600 | USB | 110g | 高级 | 呼叫中心 | $40-60 |
| H700 | 3.5mm | 95g | 中级 | 办公通用 | $30-50 |
| OBT030 | 蓝牙 | 85g | 高级 | 运动/移动办公 | $50-80 |
